About

Adam Claxon

Designer & Front End Developer

Creating websites since 1995, Adam Claxon is a designer, front-end developer, writer, and part-time philosopher.

Before starting Claxon Design Co., Adam worked at Studio Science as a designer and developer, where he had the opportunity to work with clients like Stack Overflow and Angie's List. Adam became somewhat of an advocate for accessibility, writing and speaking on the topic, and developing the company accessibility standards.
